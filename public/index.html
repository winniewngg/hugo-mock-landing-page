Open-sourced, created at the University of Pennsylvania

Seamless Assignment Sync🛡️

Never miss a deadline again! StudySync automatically fetches your assignments from Canvas and Gradescope, organizing them in one place so you don’t have to waste time manually tracking tasks. Everything stays up to date—so you can focus on studying, not scheduling.

Smart Study Schedule🚀

Your study plan, optimized in real time. StudySync adapts to your workload, deadlines, and personal schedule, making automatic adjustments when things change. Whether you need more time for a tough exam or a reminder to start early, your AI-powered planner has you covered.

Deadline & Exam Reminders📈

No more last-minute cramming! Get personalized reminders for upcoming deadlines, tests, and assignments. StudySync ensures you stay ahead of your workload by sending timely notifications, so you can prepare with confidence and avoid all-nighters.

Google Calendar Integration 🦉

Sync your academic life with your daily schedule. StudySync connects seamlessly with Google Calendar, placing all your assignments, study sessions, and deadlines in one easy-to-access location. Planning your time has never been this effortless.

AI-Powered Study Session Recommendations✨

Make every minute count. StudySync analyzes your past habits, workload, and upcoming deadlines to suggest optimal study times. Whether it’s a quick review session or a deep focus block, you’ll always know the best time to hit the books.

Task Prioritization 🧰

Tackle what matters most first. StudySync intelligently ranks your tasks based on urgency, importance, and workload, so you can focus on high-priority assignments without feeling overwhelmed. Smart planning leads to smarter studying.
